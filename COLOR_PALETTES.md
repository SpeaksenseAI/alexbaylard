# Actor Portfolio Color Strategies

Casting directors want to see your "type" quickly, so your colors should match your specific *actor branding* and harmonize with your headshots. 

Here are **4 strategic color palettes** depending on your specific casting type and the energy you want to convey.

## 🎭 Option 1: "The Leading Man" (Classic Broadway)
- **Primary:** Deep Navy Blue `#0f172a`
- **Secondary:** Rich Gold / Champagne `#d4af37`
- **Background:** Warm Off-White / Ivory `#fafafa`
- **Vibe:** Trustworthy, authoritative, and classic. Perfect if you go out for roles like *Billy Bigelow (Carousel)*, *Curly (Oklahoma)*, or principled dramatic roles.

## 🌟 Option 2: "The Contemporary Performer" (Modern & Fresh)
- **Primary:** Emerald Green `#047857`
- **Secondary:** Slate Grey `#64748b`
- **Background:** Soft Cream / Light Beige `#fefce8`
- **Vibe:** Fresh, vibrant, and approachable. Great for modern musical theatre, pop-rock shows (like *Waitress* or *Dear Evan Hansen*), and roles that require an "everyman" charm.

## 🍷 Option 3: "The Sophisticated Storyteller" (Dramatic & Intense)
- **Primary:** Charcoal / Soft Black `#18181b`
- **Secondary:** Deep Crimson / Burgundy `#9f1239`
- **Background:** Stark White `#ffffff` (or True Dark Mode)
- **Vibe:** Passionate, intense, and grounded. Ideal if you tend to book edgier, darker roles (like *Sweeney Todd*, *Phantom*, or serious straight plays).

## ⚡ Option 4: "The Triple Threat" (High Energy Pop/Rock)
- **Primary:** Vibrant Teal / Cyan `#0d9488`
- **Secondary:** Electric Tangerine / Coral `#f43f5e`
- **Background:** Dark Charcoal / Slate `#1e293b`
- **Vibe:** Dynamic, exciting, and highly energetic. Best if you are a heavy dancer, do a lot of pop/rock theatre (like *Rent*, *Rock of Ages*), or want a highly modern, stylized look.

---

### How to Choose
Look at your favorite/primary headshot. What colors are you wearing? What is the background? We want to pick a palette that makes your headshots "pop" off the screen rather than clash with them. Choose the direction that feels most like *your* personal brand.
